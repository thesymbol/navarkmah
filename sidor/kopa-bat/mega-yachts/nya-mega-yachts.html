BENETTI CLASS RANGE

The Benetti Class Range is the new Benetti Class semi-custom range of yachts from 93’ to 164’. 100 yachts have been built in the last 10 years following the first Benetti Classic model. With this background of experience, seven new models have been developed by Stefano Righini whose skilled hands are able to unite the uniqueness.
